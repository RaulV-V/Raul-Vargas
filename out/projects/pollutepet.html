PollutePet

PollutePet is a hackathon project designed to raise awareness about pollution and climate change. The app was built with Expo Go to run seamlessly on mobile devices. It pulls real-time data from NASA APIs to simulate environmental conditions, adjusting in-game temperatures and pollution levels.

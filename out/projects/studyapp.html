Study Buddy Study App

Study Buddy is a mobile study app currently in development. It helps users stay accountable by tracking how much time they spend actively studying and taking breaks. The app automatically pauses the study timer when users switch to other apps, ensuring accurate focus logging. It also records break durations to help build balanced and effective study habits. The app is built using React Native and Expo Go for fast testing on mobile devices, with local storage powered by SQLite through expo-sqlite/next. In future versions, the app will include social features like adding friends, studying together, and comparing study time stats to build healthy accountability and motivation.
